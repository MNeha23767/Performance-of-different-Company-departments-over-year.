# Performance-of-different-Company-departments-over-year.
The objective of this case study is to analyze and evaluate the performance of different departments within a company over a one-year period. The study aims to identify  the performance trends, compare departmental efficiency, and understand how various operational, financial, and human factors influence the company’s overall success. 

# Algorithm : 

Collect Data: Gather yearly performance of all departments.  

Preprocess Data: Clean,organize the data by removing missing values, formatting . 

Analyze Trends: Compare per across years and departments using descriptive statistics. 

Visualize Results: Create bar charts and line graphs to display performance variation. 

Generate Insights: Identify top-performing and low-performing departments . 

Summarize Findings: Prepare a concise report showing overall company growth .
